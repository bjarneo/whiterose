# White Rose SEO Strategy

Strategic SEO plan for White Rose, a specialist software engineering consultancy focused on technical direction, architecture review, hands-on development, DevSecOps, infrastructure, automation, reliability, and security workflows.

## Quick Start

- Keep the homepage as the brand and conversion entry point.
- Build focused service pages before publishing broad blog content.
- Use case studies, author signals, and technical proof to compete against larger consultancies.
- Track qualified organic leads, not keyword density.
- Review `SITE-STRUCTURE.md`, `CONTENT-CALENDAR.md`, `COMPETITOR-ANALYSIS.md`, and `IMPLEMENTATION-ROADMAP.md` before implementation.

## Positioning

White Rose should not try to look like a large full-service agency. The strongest SEO position is a narrow senior engineering practice with clear services, direct technical language, and proof of judgment.

Primary positioning statement:

White Rose helps teams make better software engineering decisions and implement them safely through architecture review, technical direction, development, and DevSecOps.

## Keyword Guidance

There is likely no optimal keyword density. Search engines evaluate relevance, quality, entities, links, and user satisfaction beyond repeated words.

Use keywords naturally in:

- Page title
- H1 or H2 headings
- Opening paragraph
- Service descriptions
- FAQ answers
- Internal anchor text
- Schema `name` and `description` fields

Avoid:

- Repeating exact phrases unnaturally
- Adding filler copy to chase word count
- Using keywords that do not match the actual service
- Publishing broad AI or software posts without a clear White Rose point of view

## Strategic Goals

| Goal | Why It Matters | Measurement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Build topical relevance around senior engineering advisory | Creates a clearer entity than a generic agency site | Indexed service pages, impressions, rankings |
| Convert technical buyers | SEO only matters if it creates qualified conversations | Email clicks, form submissions if added, booked calls |
| Establish E-E-A-T | Consulting buyers need trust before contact | Bio depth, case studies, named experience, references |
| Earn relevant mentions | Backlinks and citations are the current weakest signal | Referring domains, industry mentions, podcast/article citations |
| Prepare for AI search | AI systems cite clear expert pages with extractable claims | Structured pages, schema, quotable service explanations |

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
| --- | --- |
| Site stays too small | Add service pages and proof pages before broad blog content |
| Copy becomes generic | Keep language specific to architecture, delivery, DevSecOps, and implementation |
| No authority signals | Publish bio, credentials, talks, open source, client-safe case studies, and external profiles |
| No backlinks | Use targeted outreach, guest articles, technical posts, and partner references |
| Content overreach | Publish only topics White Rose can credibly own |
