# White Rose

White Rose, also styled as Whiterose, is a software engineering consultancy focused on technical direction, architecture review, hands-on development, and DevOps.

Website: https://whiterose.no

## What White Rose Does

White Rose helps teams review software systems, plan technical work, make clear engineering decisions, and deliver hands-on development and DevOps work.

Core consultancy work includes:

- Technical direction
- Architecture review
- Hands-on software development
- DevOps, infrastructure, automation, and reliability work
- Codebase, infrastructure, and process assessment

## Brand Position

White Rose represents a calm, precise, and high-craft approach to software engineering. The name is intentionally minimal and versatile, avoiding loud industry cliches while still feeling memorable and distinctive.

The brand is best suited for software consulting and architectural technical work where trust, restraint, and clear judgement matter.

## Principles

- Give advice that is simple to understand and easy to act on
- Prefer durable engineering decisions over short-term shortcuts
- Keep design clean, functional, and intentional
- Treat technical decisions as part of the product experience
- Help teams build software that feels stable, polished, and carefully made

## Visual Direction

The identity should lean toward minimal, technical elegance rather than decorative floral branding. A geometric monoline rose, restrained typography, and a clean cyber-minimalist visual system would fit the company well.

## Name Notes

White Rose is broad enough to support different kinds of software work without locking the company into a narrow niche. Because the name is common in other industries, the brand should consistently use `whiterose.no` and a precise technical presentation to keep the identity clear.
