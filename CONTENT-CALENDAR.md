# White Rose Content Calendar

Content roadmap for building topical authority around software engineering consultancy, architecture review, technical direction, development, and DevSecOps.

## Publishing Principles

- Publish fewer, better pages with direct technical value.
- Prefer service pages and proof pages before generic blog posts.
- Use natural language and avoid keyword stuffing.
- Each page needs a clear buyer intent, internal link target, and conversion path.
- Every content item should answer what White Rose does, who it helps, and when to make contact.

## Content Types

| Type | Purpose | Target Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Service page | Capture commercial intent and explain deliverables | 600-900 words |
| Proof page | Show project judgment and outcomes | 600-1,000 words |
| Insight post | Capture informational intent and demonstrate expertise | 800-1,200 words |
| Checklist | Build practical trust and AI-search-ready snippets | 700-1,000 words |
| Profile page | Build E-E-A-T and entity clarity | 500-800 words |
