Brand Overview

Company

Software engineering consultancy.

White Rose reviews software systems, plans technical work, clarifies engineering decisions, and delivers hands-on development with DevSecOps.

Services

  • 01

    Technical direction

    Help decide what to build, what to simplify, and what to avoid.

  • 02

    Architecture review

    Review systems, codebases, and infrastructure.

  • 03

    Development

    Build features, integrations, backend services, and internal tooling.

  • 04

    DevSecOps

    Support deployment, infrastructure, automation, reliability, and security workflows.

Working Model

  • Understand the system.
  • Explain the tradeoffs.
  • Help decide what to build, simplify, or avoid.
  • Support implementation when needed.
  • Keep recommendations clear and practical.

Visual System

Direction

Black, white, sharp, minimal.

The visual direction uses strong typography, clear spacing, thin borders, and the White Rose logo as the main visual mark.

Avoid gradients, color accents, soft decorative elements, stock imagery, and unnecessary effects.

Color

Black

#000000

White

#ffffff

Muted

#9a9a9a

Surface

#f4f4f4

Typography

Display

Use condensed, hard, uppercase headings. Keep headings short.

Body

Use plain monospaced or neutral sans text. Keep copy direct and factual.

Name

Use White Rose.

Use `White Rose` as the primary written name. `Whiterose` can be used as a secondary styling when needed.

The name should be tied to clear software engineering work, not floral or decorative language.

White Rose Brand guide / 02
Logo Usage

Logo

Use the existing PNG logo assets. Keep the mark square and preserve equal padding on all sides.

Rules

  • Use PNG assets only.
  • Do not stretch, skew, rotate, recolor, or crop the logo tighter.
  • Do not add shadows, glows, outlines, or effects.
  • Do not place the logo over photography or patterns.
  • Keep at least one quarter of the logo width as clear space around the mark.
